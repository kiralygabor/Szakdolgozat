MINIJOBZ — COMPLETE KNOWLEDGE BASE
====================================

ABOUT MINIJOBZ
--------------
Minijobz is a community-powered task marketplace that connects people who need everyday tasks done with local, verified Taskers who complete them. Think of it as a local marketplace where you can hire someone nearby for cleaning, handyman work, deliveries, gardening, moving, tutoring, pet care, and much more.

Minijobz was created by Király Gábor, Praszna Koppány, and Nagy Gergely.

Tagline: "Connecting people to get things done since 2025."
Currency: € (Euros)
Languages supported: English and Hungarian

Minijobz connects you with local professionals for all your home service needs. Quick, reliable, and trusted.


HOW MINIJOBZ WORKS
-------------------
Getting things done on Minijobz is simple. Here's the full step-by-step process:

Step 1: Describe what you need done
- It's free to post a task. Simply tell us what you need done, when and where you need it.
- You can upload photos to help Taskers understand the job.
- Keep it simple and clear to attract the best Taskers.
- Set a realistic budget and choose a date and time.

Step 2: Set your budget
- You're in control of your budget. Set the price you're willing to pay, or let Taskers suggest their own rates.
- Compare offers and choose the one that fits your needs and budget best.
- Don't worry, you can adjust your budget later and discuss it with potential Taskers if needed.
- Set your own price or receive quotes from Taskers.

Step 3: Receive quotes and pick the most suitable Tasker
- Receive quotes from available Taskers.
- Review their profiles, ratings, completion rates, and reviews to find the perfect match.
- Take a look at profiles, ratings, completion rate and reviews to pick the Tasker you'd like to work with.
- View Tasker profiles and reviews.
- Chat directly with Taskers.

Step 4: Secure messaging
- Once you've assigned your task, stay in contact with our secure chat to finalize details.
- Use Minijobz to stay in contact from the moment your task is posted until it's completed.
- Accept an offer and you can privately message the Tasker to discuss final details.

Step 5: Get it done
- Sit back and relax while your Tasker completes the job safely and professionally.
- When the task is complete, simply release the payment.
- Your payment is held securely by Minijobz Pay until the job is finished.

Step 6: Leave a review
- When the task is done, say thanks by releasing payment and writing a review.
- This helps the community know what a great job they've done.
- You can only leave a review after completing a task together.


POSTING A TASK
--------------
To post a task, you need to:

1. CATEGORY & DATE
   - Select a category from 13 available categories
   - Select a specific service/job within that category
   - Give your task a title (e.g., "Help move my sofa")
   - Choose when you need it done:
     * Before a specific date
     * On a specific date
     * Flexible ("I'm flexible")
   - Optionally choose a time of day: Morning (6am-12pm), Midday (12pm-3pm), Afternoon (3pm-6pm), Evening (6pm-9pm)

2. LOCATION
   - Choose how the task should be done:
     * In-person: Tasker comes to your location
     * Online: Task can be done remotely
   - For in-person tasks, enter a suburb/city

3. DETAILS
   - Provide more details so Taskers can give accurate quotes
   - Optionally add photos of your task

4. BUDGET
   - Suggest your budget (the price must be between €10 and €9,999)
   - You can always negotiate the final price
   - The price is negotiable between you and the Tasker

You can also request a quote from a specific Tasker by visiting their profile and clicking "Request a quote." The task will be sent specifically to them.


BROWSING & FINDING TASKS
-------------------------
The Browse Tasks page provides powerful tools to find the right task:

Search & Filter Options:
- Search by task name/keyword
- Filter by category (13 categories available)
- Filter by specific service/job within a category
- Filter by work type: Any, In-Person, or Remote
- Filter by location/city/suburb
- Filter by price range (€1,000 to €20,000 using a dual-range slider)
- Sort results by:
  * Most Recent
  * Closest to me
  * Due Soon
  * Price: Low to High
  * Price: High to Low

The browse page includes:
- A scrollable list of task cards showing: title, price, description, category, location, user avatar, and time posted
- An interactive map showing in-person task locations
- A "Remote Tasks" indicator showing how many tasks have no physical location
- Mobile-friendly filter modal for on-the-go browsing


TASK DETAILS PAGE
-----------------
Each task has a detail page showing:
- Task title, description, and photos
- Task budget (in €)
- Task status: Open for offers, Assigned/In Progress, or Completed
- Posted by (employer profile with avatar)
- Location information
- Due date or "Anytime" if flexible
- Number of applications/offers
- A form to make an offer (enter your offer price and a message)
- Secure payment hold notice
- Option to report the task

Before making an offer, users may be asked to complete their profile (fill in required details like location, phone number, and verification).


MY TASKS DASHBOARD
------------------
The "My Tasks" page has two views:

1. POSTED TASKS (tasks you created)
   - See all your posted tasks with status indicators:
     * "Task Posted" — sitting tight while Taskers are found
     * "New Activity / New Offers" — you have offers to review
     * "In Progress" — an offer was accepted, task is underway
     * "Task Completed" — the task is finished
   - Filter by: Posted, Pending, Completed
   - Search your tasks by name
   - View offer details and accept/decline them
   - Edit or cancel tasks
   - Mark tasks as completed when done
   - View task details: due date, location, category, job type

2. MY APPLICATIONS (tasks you offered to do)
   - See tasks where you submitted an offer
   - Status indicators:
     * "Application Sent / Waiting for response" — your offer is pending
     * "You're Hired!" — the employer accepted your offer
     * "Offer Not Selected" — the task was assigned to another Tasker
     * "Task Completed" — you completed the task
   - Contact the employer directly via messaging
   - See your offered amount and message

Direct Quote Requests:
- If someone specifically requested a quote from you, you'll see "You have a new request!"
- You can accept the current budget or send a counter offer


OFFERS & PRICING

ACCOUNT & REGISTRATION
-----------------------
Creating an account on Minijobz:
1. Go to the Sign Up page
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a password and confirm it
4. Click "Sign up"

6. After registration, complete your profile settings (name, location, etc.)

1. Go to the Login page
2. Enter your email and password

4. Click "Login"
5. You can also log in with Google or Facebook
6. Forgot password? Click "Forgot password?" to receive a reset link via email

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
----------------------------

Q: Is it free to post a task?
A: Yes! Posting a task on Minijobz is completely free. You only pay the agreed price when a Tasker completes the job.

Q: How do I pay a Tasker?
A: Payments are held securely in escrow by Minijobz Pay. When the task is completed to your satisfaction, you release the payment.

Q: Can I cancel a task?
A: Yes, you can cancel a task from your My Tasks dashboard as long as it hasn't been completed yet.

Q: How do I know a Tasker is trustworthy?
A: Check their profile for verification badges (email and phone verified), read their reviews and ratings from previous tasks, and look at their completion rate.

Q: What if I'm not happy with the work?
A: Your payment is held in escrow. If you have concerns, you can discuss them with the Tasker through secure messaging before releasing payment.

Q: Can I do tasks remotely?
A: Yes! When posting a task, you can choose "Online" as the task type. This allows Taskers to complete the work remotely without needing to visit your location.

Q: What currencies does Minijobz use?
A: Minijobz uses Euros (€) for all task pricing and payments.

Q: Is there a mobile app?
A: Yes! The Minijobz app is available on both the Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android).

Q: How do I change my password?
A: Go to your Profile settings, then click on "Security" to update your password. You'll need your old password and will set a new one.

Q: Can I delete my account?
A: Yes, you can delete your account from profile settings under "Account." Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Q: How do I report a problem?
A: You can report a task or a user directly from the task detail page or from the user's public profile. Our team will review all reports.

Q: What languages does Minijobz support?
A: Minijobz currently supports English and Hungarian. You can switch languages from the navigation bar.

Q: How do I become a Tasker?
A: Simply sign up for a free account, complete your profile, and start browsing and making offers on tasks that match your skills!

Q: Can I request a specific Tasker?
A: Yes! Visit a Tasker's public profile and click "Request a quote" to send them a direct task request.

Q: What is the price range for tasks?
A: Tasks can be priced between €10 and €9,999. When browsing, you can filter tasks by price range.
